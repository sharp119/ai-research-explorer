Key Features

Progress Tracking

Mark topics as completed and bookmark important ones to track your learning journey.

Search Functionality

Quickly find specific topics across all learning paths with real-time search results.

Difficulty Filtering

Filter learning paths by difficulty level to find content that matches your expertise.

Visual Feedback

Animations and visual cues help you navigate and understand your progress.

Resource Highlighting

Better organization of learning resources with visual hierarchy for improved navigation.

Persistent Storage

Your progress is saved in the browser's local storage between sessions.
